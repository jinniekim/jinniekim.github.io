Cheese Cake (Keto)

Don't be surprised it is too simple to make cheese cake. Simple is the best!

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese 200g
  • Sweetener 25g
  • Egg 50g (1 egg)
  • Heavy cream 50g
  • Coconut powder 20g
  • Vanilla Extract (optional) 1tsp

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven at 150C (302F).
  2. Mix all the ingredients together with a hand blender. (There is no order.)
  3. Put the batter into the baking pan and bake at 150C (302F) for 40 minutes.

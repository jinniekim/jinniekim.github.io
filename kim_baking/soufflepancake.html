Souffle Pancake

Tip: Do not open the lid too often during cooking, lower the heat and cook longer.

Ingredients

  • Flour(All purpose) 2 tbsp
  • Egg 50g
  • Sugar 1.5 tbsp
  • Milk 1 tbsp
  • Vanilla Extract 1/2 tsp
  • Lemon juice or Vinegar 3-4 drops

Directions

  1. Separate and whisk the egg white adding sugar until it turns like cloud.
  2. Combine egg yolk mixtures, egg white mixtures and flour using a flat spatula gently. (Do not use a whisk to mix.)
  3. Preheat the pan at 4/9 level for 1 minute, grease the pan.
  4. Scoop 2/3 of the batter into 2 pancakes.
  5. Add 2 tsp (room temperature) water, cover and cook 2 minutes.
  6. After 2 mins, scoop the rest of the batter into 2 pancakes.

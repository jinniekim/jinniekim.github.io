Scone

Tip : When you knead the mixture together by hand, please be careful not to work hard. This is not American style scone but English style Scone.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter 60g
  • Sugar(optional) 30g
  • Egg 50g (1 egg)
  • Flour 300g
  • Baking powder 3g
  • Milk 100g
  • Salt 1g
  • Vanilla Extract 1tsp

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven at 180C (356F).
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and chopped butter together using your fingers (more than 5 minutes).
  3. You can put a handful of dried berries in the dough if you want. (Optional)
  4. Put eggs, sugar and vanilla extract all together in milk, mix them with a fork and pour them into the dough.
    (Leave 2 to 3 spoons to apply on the scone dough later.)
  5. Mix all ingredients evenly with a spatula.
  6. Put the mixtures on the floor and knead it together by hand.
  7. Roll it out to about an inch thick with a rolling pin.
  8. Cut to desired size or cut with shape cutter.
  9. Apply the leftover milk egg mixture to the top of the scone.
  10. Bake in preheated oven at 180C (356F) for 30 minutes.

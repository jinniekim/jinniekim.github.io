Pound Cake

The basic formula is butter, sugar, egg, flour in the ratio: 1 : 1 : 1 : 1.
(Note: the butter and egg should be room temperature!)

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter 100g
  • Caster sugar 100g
  • Egg 100g
  • Cake flour 100g
  • Baking powder 2.5g
  • Heavy cream 30g
  • Salt 1g
  • Vanilla Extract 1tsp

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven at 160C (320F).
  2. Add sugar 3~4 times to the butter, whipping until the color of butter turns white and the volume increses 1.5x, adding salt.
  3. Divide the whipped eggs 3~4 times and add them gradually while you whip the butter so that the batter does not separate.
  4. Add the cake flour, baking powder and vanilla extract and fold with spatula.
  5. Add the heavy cream and fold until the batter becomes smooth.
  6. Put the batter into the baking pan, concave the center with a spatula and bake at 160C (320F) for 50 minutes.
